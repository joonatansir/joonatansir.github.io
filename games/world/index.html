Untitled world building god game (aka UWBGG)


This is a game about building a world piece by piece. You start with a single block and expand the world by choosing where and what to build next. The world's population of little dudes will increase as you place more blocks.


Currently this demo is very free-form without much explicit goals. Some future ideas:


  • Resources? Each block would produce resources that you would use to expand the world. The amount of resources a block produces would depend on what types of blocks surround it.
  • Merge mechanics? Maybe your three farms combine into a super farm to give a boost to the population of a village nearby. Maybe your villages combine into castles and castles into citadels and citadels into etc etc.
  • Multiplayer? Maybe you can build a world peacefully together or choose to destroy your enemies by building an army and strategically placing defenses.
  • All of the above?

How to play


Click a block to build next to it. Arrow keys cycles through the different block types. Hold right mouse button to rotate the view. Scroll wheel to zoom.
